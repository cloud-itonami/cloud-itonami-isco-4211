## Customer

- community banks
- credit unions
- money-service businesses

## Offer

- transaction processing
- identity verification support
- cash-drawer reconciliation
- reporting

## Revenue

- monthly teller-platform fee
- per-transaction fee
- reporting package

## Trust Controls

- no above-threshold cash disbursement without governor gate
- identity-verification overrides always require human sign-off
- transaction records are auditable, not editable
